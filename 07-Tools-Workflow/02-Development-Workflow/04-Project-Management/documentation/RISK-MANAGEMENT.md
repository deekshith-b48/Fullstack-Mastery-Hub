# Risk Management

Risk management identifies and mitigates project risks. This guide covers risk identification, assessment, and mitigation strategies.

## Risk Management Overview

Risk management:
- **Risk Identification**: Identify risks
- **Risk Assessment**: Assess risks
- **Risk Mitigation**: Mitigate risks
- **Risk Monitoring**: Monitor risks

## Risk Identification

### Risk Categories

- **Technical Risks**: Technical challenges
- **Schedule Risks**: Schedule delays
- **Resource Risks**: Resource issues
- **Scope Risks**: Scope changes
- **External Risks**: External factors

### Identification Techniques

- **Brainstorming**: Team brainstorming
- **Historical Data**: Past project data
- **Checklists**: Risk checklists
- **Expert Input**: Expert consultation
- **Stakeholder Input**: Stakeholder input

## Risk Assessment

### Risk Matrix

- **Probability**: Likelihood of occurrence
- **Impact**: Impact if occurs
- **Risk Level**: High, Medium, Low
- **Priority**: Prioritize risks

### Assessment Criteria

- **Likelihood**: How likely?
- **Impact**: What's the impact?
- **Detectability**: Can we detect it?
- **Controllability**: Can we control it?

## Risk Mitigation

### Mitigation Strategies

- **Avoid**: Avoid the risk
- **Mitigate**: Reduce impact/likelihood
- **Transfer**: Transfer to others
- **Accept**: Accept the risk

### Mitigation Plans

- **Action Items**: Specific actions
- **Owners**: Risk owners
- **Timeline**: Timeline for actions
- **Monitoring**: Monitor progress

## Best Practices

### Risk Management

1. **Early Identification**: Identify early
2. **Regular Review**: Regular risk review
3. **Documentation**: Document risks
4. **Communication**: Communicate risks
5. **Action**: Take action on risks

### Common Pitfalls

- **Ignoring Risks**: Ignoring risks
- **No Mitigation**: No mitigation plans
- **Poor Communication**: Poor communication
- **No Monitoring**: Not monitoring risks
