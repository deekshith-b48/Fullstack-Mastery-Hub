# Daily Standups

Daily Standup (Daily Scrum) is a short meeting where the team synchronizes daily. This guide covers standup purpose, format, and best practices.

## Daily Standup Overview

Daily Standup purpose:
- **Synchronization**: Synchronize team daily
- **Visibility**: Make work visible
- **Impediments**: Identify blockers
- **Alignment**: Align team on goals
- **Quick**: Short, focused meeting

## Standup Format

### Three Questions

1. **What did I do yesterday?**
   - Share completed work
   - Brief summary

2. **What will I do today?**
   - Share planned work
   - Set expectations

3. **Are there any impediments?**
   - Share blockers
   - Identify help needed

### Alternative Formats

- **Walk the Board**: Discuss board items
- **Goal-Focused**: Focus on sprint goal
- **Problem-Solving**: Quick problem-solving

## Best Practices

### Meeting Rules

- **Time-boxed**: 15 minutes maximum
- **Same Time**: Same time every day
- **Same Place**: Same location (or video call)
- **Standing**: Stand (keeps meeting short)
- **On Time**: Start on time

### Participation

- **Everyone Attends**: All team members
- **Brief**: Keep answers brief
- **Focused**: Focus on work, not status
- **Respectful**: Respect others' time

## Common Issues

### Problems to Avoid

- **Status Report**: Don't make it a status report
- **Too Long**: Keep under 15 minutes
- **Discussion**: Don't discuss in detail
- **Management Meeting**: Not for management updates

### Solutions

- **Stick to Format**: Follow three questions
- **Time-box**: Enforce time limits
- **Take Offline**: Move discussions offline
- **Focus**: Focus on team coordination

## Facilitation

### Scrum Master Role

- **Facilitate**: Facilitate meeting
- **Time-keeper**: Keep time
- **Focus**: Keep focus on coordination
- **Impediments**: Track impediments

### Team Self-Organization

- **Team Runs**: Team runs own standup
- **Self-Facilitate**: Rotate facilitator
- **Ownership**: Team owns meeting

## Remote Standups

### Virtual Standups

- **Video Call**: Use video conferencing
- **Screen Share**: Share board if needed
- **Same Format**: Use same format
- **Time Zone**: Consider time zones

### Tools

- **Video**: Zoom, Teams, Google Meet
- **Board**: Share digital board
- **Chat**: Use chat for follow-ups

## Benefits

### Team Benefits

- **Visibility**: Work is visible
- **Coordination**: Better coordination
- **Impediments**: Quick blocker identification
- **Alignment**: Team stays aligned
